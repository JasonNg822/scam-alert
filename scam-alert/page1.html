Cyber Scams in Singapore

Cyber scams are surging in Singapore, with cases jumping 49.6% to 50,376 in 2023. As we become more digital, scammers are getting smarter and more aggressive.

Common scams include online shopping fraud, romance scams, job scams, and government impersonation. Government Official Impersonation Scams alone cost Singaporeans S$151.3 million in 2024.



These scams target everyone – 70.9% of victims in 2024 were under 50 years old. Scammers use social media and messaging apps to make their approaches feel personal and trustworthy.

The problem is regional too, with Asian consumers losing nearly $700bn to digital scams in 2024.



Protection starts with awareness. Know the warning signs, stay informed about new tactics, and practice safe digital habits to protect yourself and your family.

Stay alert. Stay safe.
