How can I spot a scam?

The most common scam is unsolicited calls, emails, or messages asking for money or personal details.

What should I do if I've been scammed?

Stop all contact with the scammer then report it to authorities and warn others to prevent further victims.

How can I protect myself from scams?

Never share passwords, PINs, or verification codes and verify unexpected requests by contacting the company directly.

Can I get my money back if I was scammed?

It depends, but recovery is difficult. If you paid via credit or debit card you need dispute the charge with your bank. If Bank transfer, you need contact your bank immediately. If gift cards or crypto that is nearly impossible to recover.
